Margins, Borders, Padding09-21-2015


The content refers to everything in the pink colored box. Whether images or words fill that box- the pink is the content.

Padding is the extra space between the content and border. Padding is always transparent.

The border refers to the space between the padding and the margin. The border can be however thick you want it (from 0 pixels to 100+ pixels). As a developer, you can choose the color, gradient, and thickness of the border.

The margin refers to the white space between the border of this content and the border of the content around it. The border is always transparent.
